Egg Fried Rice

Description

Elevate your culinary experience with the enticing flavors of homemade egg fried rice. This recipe takes perfectly cooked white rice and transforms it into a delectable masterpiece. Begin by preparing the rice and allowing it to cool, ensuring its optimal texture when combined with other ingredients. Whisk eggs into a savory scramble and set them aside. In a well-heated wok, create a symphony of aromas by sautéing finely chopped onion and fragrant minced garlic. Introduce a medley of colorful vegetables to the mix, infusing the dish with both nutrition and vibrant hues. As the vegetables soften, make room for the cooled rice to sizzle in the pan, achieving a tantalizing crispness. The rice becomes a canvas for the rich flavors of soy sauce and aromatic sesame oil, harmonizing with the sautéed components. Reunite the scrambled eggs with the ensemble, each bite a celebration of diverse textures and tastes. A sprinkle of green onions imparts a refreshing finish, elevating the visual and gustatory appeal. Whether savored as a standalone delight or served alongside other Asian favorites, this egg fried rice guarantees a journey of flavors that dance in harmony on your palate.

Ingredients

  • Cooked and cooled white rice
  • Eggs
  • Vegetables (such as diced carrots, peas, and corn)
  • Onion, finely chopped
  • Garlic, minced
  • Soy sauce
  • Sesame oil
  • Salt and pepper
  • Green onions, chopped (for garnish)

Steps

  1. Cook the white rice according to package instructions. Spread it on a tray to cool down.
  2. Whisk the eggs with a pinch of salt and pepper. Heat a wok or pan, add oil, and scramble the eggs until just set. Set aside.
  3. In the same pan, sauté finely chopped onion until translucent. Add minced garlic and cook for 30 seconds.
  4. Push onion and garlic to the side. Add diced vegetables and stir-fry until slightly softened.
  5. Push vegetables aside. Add cooled rice to the pan. Break up clumps and let it cook for a few minutes.
  6. Drizzle soy sauce and sesame oil over the rice. Toss to combine.
  7. Add scrambled eggs back to the pan and mix.
  8. Season with salt and pepper. Stir-fry for a couple more minutes.
  9. Turn off the heat and garnish with chopped green onions.
  10. Your egg fried rice is ready to be enjoyed as a flavorful meal or side dish!
